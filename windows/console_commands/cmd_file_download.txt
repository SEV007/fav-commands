BITSadmin

Examples:

bitsadmin /transfer myDownloadJob /download /priority normal http://downloadsrv/10mb.zip c:\10mb.zip

bitsadmin /addfile myDownloadJob http://downloadsrv/10mb.zip c:\10mb.zip

bitsadmin /resume myDownloadJob

bitsadmin /list
